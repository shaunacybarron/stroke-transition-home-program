Purpose of Application

This application supports the Stroke Transition Home Program by securely accessing Oura Ring data from participants who have provided informed consent. The application is used to generate participant activity reports and support program monitoring and research activities.

Authorized Use

Access to this application is limited to authorized personnel involved in the Stroke Transition Home Program. Users may only access participant data for approved purposes and in accordance with applicable research protocols and consent procedures.

Data Access and Use

Oura data are accessed through the Oura API only after participant authorization. Data are used solely for the purposes described in the Stroke Transition Home Program and are not sold or used for commercial purposes.

Data Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect participant information. Access to data is restricted to authorized individuals.

Availability and Changes

The application is provided for research and program purposes. Features, functionality, or access may be modified as required to support the goals of the Stroke Transition Home Program.

Contact

For questions regarding this application or its use, please contact the Stroke Transition Home Program research team.
