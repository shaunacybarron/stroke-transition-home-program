Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the Stroke Transition Home Program Oura Application accesses and uses information collected through Oura Ring devices. The application is designed to support research and program activities for participants who have provided informed consent.

Information Collected

With participant authorization, this application accesses data provided through the Oura API. Information accessed may include activity, sleep, heart rate, and other Oura-derived health and wellness metrics required for the purposes of the Stroke Transition Home Program activity reports and activity counselling.

Purpose of Data Use

Oura data are used to generate participant activity reports and support monitoring and activity counselling associated with the Stroke Transition Home Program. Data are used only for approved program and research purposes.

Consent and Authorization

Participant data are accessed only after participants provide informed consent and authorize the application to access their Oura data through the Oura API. Participants may withdraw authorization according to the procedures outlined in the applicable research consent process.

Data Sharing

Participant information is not sold, rented, or shared for commercial purposes. Data are only accessible to authorized members of the Stroke Transition Home Program research team or other individuals approved according to applicable research procedures.

Data Security

Appropriate administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards are used to protect participant information. Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel involved in approved program and research activities.

Data Retention

Data are retained and managed according to the requirements of the Stroke Transition Home Program and applicable institutional research policies.

Third-Party Services

This application uses the Oura API to access participant-authorized Oura data. Information accessed through the Oura API is handled in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable research requirements.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the use of Oura data within the Stroke Transition Home Program, please contact the research team.
